Our Mission

Why Green? Sylene Energy Environmental Consulting is dedicated to charging towards a sustainable future by providing expert guidance and information on clean, reliable green energy, empowering clients to make informed decisions that contribute to a greener world.


Timeline

September 2006

Sylene Energy was founded on September 18, 2006 by CEO Vivaan Singhvi. The executive board quickly expanded to include COO Jesse Hao, CFO Grace Feng, and CIO Caleb Han.

January 2008

Sylene Energy became a venture company under the newly formed Sylene Group, further dedicated into the improvement of this Earth. Sister venture companies such as Sylene Electric Automobiles and Sylene Aerospace develop to become pioneers in their industries.

June 2011

Sylene Energy hires experienced CTO Flo Mingo for experienced advice. This new board member brings sweeping reform to the technology networkers and backend developers. Company efficiency skyrockets with this new change.

March 2016

Sylene Energy announces official cooperation with AECOM, Tetra Tech, and Jacobs Solutions, other pioneers in the environmental consulting industry. This unexpected cooperation intends to improve the Earth's energy situation for the greater good.

Today

Sylene Energy continues to be a driving advocate for the research, implementation, and development of renewable energy sources. Sylene Energy will continue to believe in the importance in expert guidance for anyone, improving the Earth little by little.
