MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to make safe, portable, electric coolers to transport COVID-19 vaccines to remote third-world countries in need, and ultimately, everyone. Keeping the vaccines in suitable conditions for extended amounts of time while maintaining use and efficiency, the coolers bring hope and safety. This also allows the people to be empowered and freed from the restrictions that the virus brought.

Background

Our team came across this idea while doing some research on COVID-19. We realized that vaccines needed to stay in certain temperatures, but in order to get these vaccines to third world countries the planes required many stops in order to charge the cooler. We figured out that we could make our own product using renewable energy so that cooler go could the entire way without stopping.

UN Goals

Our services meet the expectations of the Third UN Sustainable Development Goal (Good Health and Well-Being) to ensure quality and safety. In addition, the Seventh UN Sustainable Development Goal (Affordable and Clean Energy) is also met.
