FAQ

  • Q: Why do you have to have 2 sources of where you accept donations?

    A: We ensure that whatever donation that goes to coolers will 100% be for coolers. Whatever is for staffing, we give some to coolers and some for staffing.

  • Q: How did you make your organization?

    A: We came across this problem with that vaccines had to be in certain temperatures, but many third world countries did not have access to them. Therefore, we started this organization.

  • Q: How can I support other than donating?

    A: View the CDC website on how to be safe from the virus! This helps you and others have a less chance of getting infected.

  • Q: What will we do after the COVID-19 virus goes away?

    A: Our team will still produce these coolers for other vaccines, but our focus will be directed to other projects.
